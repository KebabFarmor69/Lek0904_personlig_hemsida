Hobbies:

Art

Art came to me when I was a little girl, wanting to express my creativity. I started of with aquarelle, as that was what my grandmother worked with at the moment. Later on, as I developed my skills, I began my journey with acrylics, which most of my paintings are nowadays.

Sports

For about 5 years I've played handball in AIK and Spånga HK. For the past year, I changed to Spånga HK because of personal issues. I find it relaxing and refreshing to work out, since it makes me feel strong and healthy.

Gaming

For most of my life, I've played video games. It varies from First-Person-Shooters, to games like The Sims or Cities Skyline. Gaming helps my motivation grow and makes me inspired to create different artpieces or relax and put my mind of of difficult things.
